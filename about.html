About Reka's Corner

Let's Work Together!

Reka's Corner believes in the power of digital marketing. It's proven that digital marketing has a massive impact on businesses.

Digital marketing is a kind of marketing that uses the internet. It uses digital technologies such as desktops, laptops, mobile phones or tablets, and other digital media to promote products and services. Nowadays, using digital platforms in marketing plans has been successful and effective.

People usually use digital devices instead of visiting physical shops, like shopping or looking for services they need. Digital marketing campaigns have become standard for businesses, and it is suitable for start-ups. Search Engine Optimization or SEO is part of digital marketing.

Search engine optimization is ranking your business website in search engines like Google, Yahoo, Bing, etc. In doing so, it will improve your website's organic traffic.

There are two kinds of SEO practices, white hat SEO and Black hat SEO. Google only wants White hat SEO, and if Google detects that you are using a Black hat strategy, the website you want to rank will get penalized.

White hat SEO is the best practice in ranking your website. Doing white hat SEO means SEO specialist follows the rules and terms of search engines and improves your domain authority or domain rating. Black SEO is the reverse practice, and it is against search engine rules.

The benefit of SEO to your business is the worldwide visibility of the products or services you offer. It is an inbound marketing strategy. It is for your prospective audience to find your products or services easily.

If your business is in a higher rank of SERP or search engine result page, your site will generate more clicks, traffic, and customers.

SEO also improves UI or user interface, which leads to prospect buyers, buyers, or repeat buyers if you are selling products. If you are offering services, putting some stars in your reviews will attract more clients. SEO specialist knows how to do it.

An SEO specialist is a person who is an expert in doing SEO. The specialist will analyze, improves, and optimize your site. He/She will implement a white hat strategy so that your website will get more organic traffic and a better SERP ranking.

Also, an errorless website has good benefits. It is one of the ranking factors. An SEO specialist who knows Web Development is a plus factor for you as a client. He/She will check the technical errors of the website.

Web Developer will make sure your website is using HTTPS. They will improve your site speed and make it mobile-friendly. The correct redirections, removing broken links, and 404 will enhance your core web vital metrics. The web developer will handle the overall technicality of your website for UX/UI.
