What is Site Audit?

Site Audit is the process of scanning or crawling the website for SEO errors using tools. This tools will give metrics like traffic and ranking. It will give an overview analysis of the website, show the site health score, user experience, and user engagement. It will also indicate website performance and website errors that affect the ranking of the websites.

The site audit will give you a clearer vision of your goals. It will also provide ideas on what strategy or techniques are best.

The benefits of site audit are:

  • It will make your website performance faster.
  • It will show your ranking keywords or ranking page.
  • It will show your current ranking or position in SERP.
  • It will show how your competitors are doing on their SEO.
  • It will give ideas on how you make your content better and stronger.
  • It will show you possible conversion rates.
  • It will show you an idea of how to earn on traffic trends.
  • It will show how you improve or implement the correct usage of keywords.
  • It will show you if your site has duplicate or canonical.
  • It will show if your site has HTTP/HTTPS errors.
  • It will show if your robots.txt blocking the search engine bots.

An SEO Specialists do this method when analyzing the problems of the website. It will give them complete insight into any website traffic and individual pages. It is needed to improve Search Engine Optimization (SEO).
