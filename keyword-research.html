What is Keyword Research?

It is the process of researching popular words or phrases. Keyword research aims to find the keywords that people usually type when looking for a product or service in a search engine like Google.

SEO Specialists include the words or phrases in the content. It may be Blog content, news article, etc.

If the SEO specialist use the correct strategy in implementing the keywords, these words or phrases will appear higher on a search engine result page or SERP.

Keyword research use tools to see if the keyword is easy, medium, or hard to rank.

We use paid and free tools in doing keyword research. These tools are Ahrefs, Semrush, Ubbersuggest, Keyword Finder, and Google Keyword Planner.
