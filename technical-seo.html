What is Technical Search Engine Optimization?

Technical SEO is the process of optimizing your website to help search engines like Google to find, understand, and index your pages.

It refers to optimizing your site for crawling and indexing. It can also include any technical process meant to improve search visibility.

In Technical SEO, it makes sure that the website has HTTPS and SSL. It means that your website is secure.

Also, check for a duplicate version of the website. Find and fix crawl errors and make the website mobile-friendly.

Technical SEO is a broad and exciting field. If implemented correctly, it will help your website rank on the top page of SERP.
